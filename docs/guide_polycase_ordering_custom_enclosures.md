# Guide: Re-ordering Custom Enclosures from Polycase

Polycase does not allow the re-ordering of custom parts by different users (unless the accounts are added to a master company account). I verified this directly with Polycase Support.<br>

You will have to create a **new custom part** under your user account using the same CAD data as the original custom part. This will generate a **new custom part number** associated with _your user account_ that will make it easy to reorder more custom enclosures in the future.

1. Visit the [**Polycase Instant Quote page**](https://www.polycase.com/quote)
      * Log-in or Create a User Account

1. **Select a Product**:
      * **+ New Customized Enclosure**
      * Series: **DC Series**
      * Product: **DC-45F**
      * Color: **Gray**
      * Cover Style: **Recessed / Smooth**
      * Click **Continue**<br>

    <img src="/img/polycase_select_product.png" alt="polycase_select_product.png" width="600">

1. **Machining**:
      * Machining Base
        * Surfaces: **2**
        * Total Cutouts: **8**
      * Machining Cover
        * Surfaces: **1**
        * Total Cutouts: **10**
      * Click **Continue**<br>

    <img src="/img/polycase_machining.png" alt="polycase_machining.png" width="400">

1. **Printing**:
      * _No direct-printing in this version, use sticker instead_
      * Click **Get Quote**

1. Once the **Quote** is generated, expand the **Upload Drawings** section, Click **Browse Files**, and upload the following files:
      * [<img src="/img/pdf_icon.png" alt="pdf_icon.png" width="15"> **24592a.pdf**](hardware/mech/polycase_drawings/24592a.pdf)
      * [<img src="/img/pdf_icon.png" alt="pdf_icon.png" width="15"> **24592ab.pdf**](hardware/mech/polycase_drawings/24592ab.pdf)
      * [<img src="/img/pdf_icon.png" alt="pdf_icon.png" width="15"> **24592ac.pdf**](hardware/mech/polycase_drawings/24592ac.pdf)<br>

      <img src="/img/polycase_upload_drawings.png" alt="polycase_upload_drawings.png" width="600">

1. Select **QTY**, **Expedite Options** and Click **Add to Cart**

1. Polycase staff will review the Custom Part info and reply within 1-2 business days with approval, or to ask for more information.

1. Once a Custom Part has been completed under your User Account, it's easy to reorder more by going to<br>**My Account > My Quotes & Custom Parts > [Custom Part #] > Reorder**<br><img src="/img/polycase_reorder.png" alt="polycase_reorder.png" width="600">
